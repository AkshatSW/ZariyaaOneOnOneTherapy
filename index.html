What Are One-On-One Therapy Sessions?

One-on-one healing therapy is a magical journey towards a happier and healthier you. It helps you meet with a therapist and discuss your problems and learn a new approach to tackle them effectively. Healing therapy can be utilised for both personal and professional purposes.

Why do you need one on one healing therapy?

Our journey through life is often riddled with so many questions. Questions about career, relationships, work-life balance and even ourselves, this therapy can help you in a very positive and constructive manner.

It can help boost performance, reduce stress, anxiety, burnout, break the limiting beliefs hindering your growth and hand you the key to unlock the door to receive all the goodness and abundance that the universe has to offer to you.

Therapy Is For Everyone

Counselling is for Everyone And anybody who is troubled by a short-term, transient difficult period. Or for someone who is coping with a chronic, long-term issue that is taking its toll on them. Many individuals contact a therapist at least once in their life, and there are several reasons why people seek therapy. If you're thinking, "Why do individuals seek counselling?" here are a few reasons why you might believe this is a smart choice -

If you wish to experience greater joy in their life.

If you wish to improve your sexual life.

If you wish to have more stable and/or close relationships.

If you wish to feel better at ease in their lives.

If you wish to become more self-aware.

If you want to learn more about yourself and understand yourself better.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is one on one therapy?

In one on one therapy, you will get assigned one therapist available just for you, and you’ll be able to privately discuss your problem.

How does it work?

Each therapist works with you to set achievable goals and develop an action plan. All therapists are professional therapists who work throughout the country. These professionals are also licensed to practice in their state of residence.

Who can take one on one therapy?

Everyone! We even encourage you to try one on one therapy even if you believe you don’t need it.

How do I enrol?

Enrol by filling the form by clicking the book now button and we’ll take care of everything else.

What if I have any problem?

You can contact your therapist on and off the session.

How can I contact you?

For any queries that you may have, you can contact us at happy@zariyaa.in
